Your working memory is being hijacked

When you ask Gemini a hard question, it takes 15–30 seconds to respond. In that gap, your brain seeks stimulation — you switch to YouTube or Reddit "just for a second."

By the time Gemini finishes, you're 3 videos deep. The mental context of your original question? Gone. You've lost 15 minutes and have to rebuild your train of thought from scratch.

Focus Boomerang closes that gap. The moment Gemini finishes generating, it snaps your focus back — but only if you wandered to a distraction. If you're reading notes, it leaves you alone.

🗂

Three types of tabs

You decide what each site is. Focus Boomerang respects your judgment.

📚 Study

Productive tabs. You'll get a gentle notification when Gemini finishes, but no force-switch. Examples: hello.iitk.ac.in, NotebookLM, lecture slides.

🐇 Rabbit Hole

Interesting but risky tabs. You'll get a gentle ping. If you keep going back, the extension escalates to a stronger nudge. Examples: Wikipedia, Hacker News.

🚫 Distraction

Known time-sinks. When Gemini finishes and you're here, you're forcefully switched back — with a full-screen overlay. Examples: YouTube (memes), Reddit, Instagram.

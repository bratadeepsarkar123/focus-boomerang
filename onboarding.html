Welcome to Focus Boomerang

Ever ask an AI a question, open a new tab while you wait, and then get completely lost in a distraction?

Focus Boomerang solves this by watching Gemini generate your answer and instantly pulling your focus back when it's done.

Let's set up your focus tiers so the extension knows how to handle different websites.

Classify: Study Domains

These are places where you do actual work or research. When you're on a Study domain, the extension will just send a quiet notification when Gemini finishes.

One domain per line, or comma-separated.
Tracking: None

Classify: Rabbit Hole Domains

These are neutral sites that can be productive but often trap you (like news, blogs, or forums). Unclassified tabs default to this.

The extension sends a gentle notification here. But if you dismiss it 5 times or stay over 5 minutes, it escalates to a force-switch.

One domain per line, or comma-separated. Note: YouTube is generally a distraction, but if you watch lectures, you might put it here.
Tracking: None

Classify: Distraction Domains

These are the ultimate time-wasters. If you are on one of these domains when Gemini finishes, Focus Boomerang will violently pull you back to the AI tab immediately.

One domain per line, or comma-separated.
Tracking: None
